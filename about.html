About Us

Inventrix

Inventrix is the ultimate celebration of innovation and imagination at the IT Department of K.E.T.S V.G. Vaze College. It’s where bright minds come together to turn ideas into reality, compete in electrifying events, and explore the latest in technology. Bursting with creativity, energy, and collaboration, Inventrix is more than a fest—it’s an experience that inspires, challenges, and ignites the passion of every student.

IT Department

The IT Department at K.E.T.S V.G. Vaze College is a hub of innovation and creativity, where curious minds explore the limitless possibilities of technology. With a perfect blend of learning, experimentation, and collaboration, the department empowers students to push boundaries, solve real-world challenges, and turn bold ideas into groundbreaking solutions. It’s more than a department—it’s a launchpad for the tech leaders of tomorrow.

Faculty
